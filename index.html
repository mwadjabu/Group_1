About us

This Thursday June, 13th 2024, Chief Digital Officers were gathered to celebrate their impact across various sectors in accelerating Rwanda’s digital transformation. The event brought together representatives from the Ministry of ICT and Innovation, Rwanda Information Society Authority, Agence Française de Développement, Expertise France, other partners and all CDOs.
